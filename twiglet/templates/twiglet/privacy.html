Twiglet Software's Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to Twiglet Software. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that Twiglet Software collects, and the ways in which Twiglet Software uses that personal information.

About this privacy statement

This privacy statement was made using a Contractology privacy statement document available at www.freenetlaw.com. You can purchase a wide range of commercial legal documents via the Contractology website.

Personal information collection

Twiglet Software may collect and use the following kinds of personal information:

  • We collect name and mail address information when you download a trial version of our software so that we can follow up later. You will not receive further unsolicited emails other than this follow up message.
  • We collect name and mail address information when you use the contact form to ask for information. You will not receive any further marketing other than a reply to your request.

Information about Cookies and about Google Analytics

Cookies are very small text files stored on your computer by your web browser.

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. (Google Privacy Policies) which uses javascript and cookies to help us analyze how visitors use the site.

We also use cookies to improve ease of use of this site. For example, to remember the layout of pages between visits.

You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser. If cookies are refused, you will still be able to use our website, and anonymous data will still be collected in our internal transaction logs. By accepting cookies, you consent to the processing of data in the manner and for the purposes set out in this privacy notice.

Using personal information

Twiglet Software may use your personal information to:

  • personalize the website for you;
  • make a one off follow up up email following a trial download.

Where Twiglet Software discloses your personal information to its agents or sub-contractors for these purposes, the agent or sub-contractor in question will be obligated to use that personal information in accordance with the terms of this privacy statement.

In addition to the disclosures reasonably necessary for the purposes identified elsewhere above, Twiglet Software may disclose your personal information to the extent that it is required to do so by law, in connection with any legal proceedings or prospective legal proceedings, and in order to establish, exercise or defend its legal rights.

Securing your data

Twiglet Software will take reasonable technical and organisational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information.

Controlling your personal information

If you have submitted individually identifiable information through Twiglet Software's Web site and would like that information deleted from our records, please contact us at privacy@twigletsoftware.com.

Cross-border data transfers

Information that Twiglet Software collects may be stored and processed in and transferred between any of the countries in which Twiglet Software operates to enable the use of the information in accordance with this privacy policy.

You agree to such cross-border transfers of personal information.

Updating this statement

Twiglet Software may update this privacy policy by posting a new version on this website.

You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are familiar with any changes.

Other websites

This website contains links to other websites.

Twiglet Software is not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of any third party.

Contact Twiglet Software

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or Twiglet Software's treatment of your personal information, please write:

  • by email to privacy@twigletsoftware.com
