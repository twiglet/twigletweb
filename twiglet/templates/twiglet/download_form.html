Download a trial version of CS2J and try it on your own code. The trial version has three restrictions, compared to the full version:
  • It just shows the first 120 lines of each class.
  • Each translation template is digitally signed. You can add and / or modify up to five of the translation templates.
  • The trial version is usually a little out of date, and is missing features and bug fixes from the full version
The trial version will give you a good idea of the quality of translation you can expect from the full CS2J product and allows you to play with the translation templates.

To download the trial version just provide your name, email address and (optionally) a short description of your translation needs, then the download will start straight away.
